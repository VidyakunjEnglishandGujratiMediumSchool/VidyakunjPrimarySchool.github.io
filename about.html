About Trust

Krisha Education and Charitable Trust, located in Dolvan, provides primary education to students via Vidyakunj Primary School. The Trust strives to provide quality education to children from 1st to 8th grade, with a focus on preparing them for success in higher education and in their future careers. We are committed to making a difference in the lives of children and families in Dolvan and beyond.

Trust's primary responsibilities include building and developing a strategic plan, serving as fiduciary of the School, and maintaining and employing the Head of School.
