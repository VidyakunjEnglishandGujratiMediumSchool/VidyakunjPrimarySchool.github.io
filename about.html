About our School

Vidyakunj Primary School, which is owned and run by vidyakunj Trust – Dolvan, has been working to mould young minds since 2014. Its only objective is to give kids a holistic education with integrated learning and to encourage their creativity.

Vidyakunj Primary School endeavors to reach out and contribute in nurturing responsible citizens of tomorrow, through quality education and a learning environment that is student-friendly yet intellectually inspiring. Vidyakunj Primary School (VPS) solely aims at providing education that goes beyond the classroom and want our children to be critical thinkers, problem solvers, lifelong learners and gratified individuals of a responsible society. VPS is a co-educational English and Gujarati Medium School Dolavan. It aims to provide holistic education with integrated learning and imbibe creative thinking in children. At VPS we have an experienced and specialized team of Educators who indoctrinate lifelong preparedness through leadership education to meet the global challenges of the future.

Vision

Shaping a better future for mankind by developing effective and socially responsible individuals.

Mission

The education initiative emphasizes the all-round development of its students. It aims at producing not only good professionals, but also good and worthy citizens of a great country, aiding in its overall progress and development. It endeavours to treat every student as an individual, to recognise their potential and to ensure that they receive the best preparation and training for achieving their career ambitions and life goals.

The School Campus

The school has serene campus in dolvan Mota faliyu, where Classes from Class I to Class VIII are running successfully. The state-of-art campus is equipped with large spaces for daily assembly and varied curricular / co-curricular activities and administration functions, including various support functions such as Labs, Libraries, Music Room, Dance area, Amphitheater, etc. The classrooms are spacious and well-ventilated, with necessary lighting .
