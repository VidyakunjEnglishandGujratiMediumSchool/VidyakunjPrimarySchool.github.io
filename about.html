About Trust

Vidyakunj Primary School (VPS) was established with a vision to nurture responsible citizens of tomorrow through quality education and a learning environment that is student-friendly yet intellectually inspiring. The school goal is to provide education beyond the classroom and to turn their students into critical thinkers, problem solvers, lifelong learners and gratified individuals of a responsible society.

The school has created a conducive environment for learning and development, where students are encouraged to express their thoughts and opinions freely. Learning is not confined to classrooms and the school focuses on extra-curricular activities and field trips to engage the students.

The teachers and staff members at VPS take their roles seriously and guide the students with utmost care as they are the custodians of the future. They strive to instill the values of honesty, integrity, respect and empathy in their students.

The school also has a robust system of monitoring and evaluation to ensure the personal growth of each student. The assessments and feedback system help the teachers and administrators to understand the strengths and weaknesses of the students and work towards their overall development.
