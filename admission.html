Admission Process

  • Normally our academic year starts from June.parents who want their ward to get admission in our school need to contact the office.
  • After collecting the 'Admission form' and filling up the required details the form has to be returned to the office again and then know about the specific date for interview from the office.
  • After undergoing the interview procedure, the parents can collect the information about the child's eligibility for admission on the very next day.
  • If the child is eligible, the admission will be confirmed after the payment of fees only. Later the parents would be personally informed about the books, uniform, timings and such other required information & details about the school academics.

Age Criteria

  • As per the CBSE rules, to secure admissions into class 1, the minimum age requirement is 6 years and the maximum age is 7 years. Parents should calculate the age of a learner until March of the year they are applying in.
  • Age eligibility criteria for seeking admission in Early Year Program is minimum 3 years and in Grade I is 6 years as on 31st March.

Mandatory Documents Required

  • Original Birth Certificate + Photo copy of the same
  • Photo copy of Aadhar card of learner + both Parents
  • Photo copy of Address Proof
  • Copy of previous school results if any & No dues certificate
  • 3 Photos of learner + Both Parents
  • Original Leaving/Transfer Certificate + Copy of the same (in case of tranfer)
